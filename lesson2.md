## Why Are Highly Processed Foods Generally Less Healthy?
- Food processing remove important nutrients like iron, fiber and VB
- Purpose: soft texture and longer shelf life (microbes less attracted because of low nutrient)
- Sugar, salt, and fat added to make nutrient striped food tasty, but raise calories
- **Nutrient density** = nutritional value / energy content
- Fast food cheaper because they are energy dense, but not nutrient dense
